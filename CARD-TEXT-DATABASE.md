# 📋 Veiled Dominion: Card Text Database

This document contains the official rules text for all 52 cards in the Veiled Dominion deck. This text is final and should be copied exactly into the card generator (nanDECK) or design files.

## Universal Face Cards (J, Q, K, A)
*These cards share the same text across all four suits.*

### J — The Lieutenant
**Deploy a Pawn** one square forward on the board (pre-staging) immediately. Works for any suit.

### Q — The Strategist
**Draw 3 cards**, keep 2, discard 1. Intelligence wins wars before they start. Works for any suit.

### K — The Warlord
**Double your next attack**, OR gain **Iron Resolve**: immune to 1 capture in Phase III. Works for any suit.

### A — The Wild Fate
Declare any suit. Use as pip 1 or 14. Or hold as **Last Stand**: nullify 1 check in Phase III. Works for any suit.
