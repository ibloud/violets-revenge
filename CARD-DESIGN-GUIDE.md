# 🃏 Veiled Dominion: Card Design Guide (Violet's Revenge Edition)

This document outlines the art direction, visual identity, and layout specifications for the *Veiled Dominion* card deck, themed specifically for the *Violet's Revenge* universe.

This deck recontextualizes the war game as the supernatural "Purge" happening inside Violet's purgatory morgue. The suits are divided into Violet's forensic tools and the Guilty's desperate survival tactics.

## 1. Overall Visual Identity
*   **Aesthetic:** "Gothic Forensics." A blend of clinical morgue sterility and supernatural horror. Think crime scene investigation meets tarot cards.
*   **Background Texture:** Dark, weathered industrial tile (like a morgue wall) with faint, glowing purple Luminol splatters and chalk outlines bleeding through the edges.
*   **Typography:**
    *   *Titles/Names:* A sharp, aggressive serif font (e.g., *Cinzel* or *Trajan Pro*), resembling engraved tombstones.
    *   *Rules Text:* A clean, clinical monospace font (e.g., *Courier New* or *Roboto Mono*) to mimic typewritten police/coroner reports. Keywords (Attack str., Block str., RP) are bolded in forensic blue or crimson.

## 2. The Four Suits (Color Palettes & Motifs)

### ♠ SPADES — Conquest (Attack)
*   **Thematic Domain:** *The Executioner's Hunt.* Violet taking vengeance.
*   **Color Palette:** Supernatural Purple & Crimson.
*   **Icon Motif:** Hockey sticks, autopsy scalpels, barbed wire, the number 88.
*   **Visual Vibe:** Aggressive, jagged, violent. Art should feature Violet striking from the shadows or her supernatural purple energy tearing through the Guilty.

### ♥ HEARTS — Restoration (Support)
*   **Thematic Domain:** *The Autopsy Slab.* Survival, medical intervention, and tragic memories.
*   **Color Palette:** Clinical White & Pale Gold.
*   **Icon Motif:** Morgue freezers, first-aid kits, white candles, the unfinished Nightingale painting.
*   **Visual Vibe:** Cold, sterile, desperate. Art should feature the Guilty patching themselves up with stolen medical supplies or Violet's lingering memories of Jenny.

### ♦ DIAMONDS — Intelligence (Subterfuge)
*   **Thematic Domain:** *Forensic Investigation.* Gathering evidence, setting traps, and the Guilty trying to cover their tracks.
*   **Color Palette:** Luminol Blue & Morgue Steel.
*   **Icon Motif:** Magnifying glasses, case files, Nightingale audio traps, glowing footprints.
*   **Visual Vibe:** Sleek, shadowy, analytical. Art should feature glowing evidence, peeping through morgue doors, or intercepted radio chatter.

### ♣ CLUBS — Fortification (Defense)
*   **Thematic Domain:** *The Morgue Walls.* Barricading against the supernatural.
*   **Color Palette:** Bruised Black & Heavy Iron.
*   **Icon Motif:** Autopsy tables (used as shields), heavy freezer doors, chained hockey goals, grave dirt sachets.
*   **Visual Vibe:** Heavy, claustrophobic, industrial. Art should feature the Guilty hiding under steel tables or Violet's traps being fortified.

## 3. Card Anatomy (Layout Blueprint)
Every card should look like a top-secret forensic case file.

1.  **Top Bar (The Header):**
    *   *Left:* The Suit Icon (stylized: ♠ as a hockey stick blade, ♥ as a heart monitor pulse, ♦ as an evidence tag, ♣ as a morgue freezer handle).
    *   *Center:* The Card Name (e.g., "VANGUARD CHARGE", "PHOENIX RITE").
    *   *Right:* The Pip Value (2-10, J, Q, K, A) stamped in red ink like a coroner's stamp.
2.  **Mid-Section (The Art Box):**
    *   A framed illustration framed by police tape or morgue tile.
3.  **Lower-Mid (The Domain Tag):**
    *   A small evidence tag stating the domain (e.g., "CONQUEST", "INTELLIGENCE").
4.  **Bottom Box (The Rules Text):**
    *   The mechanical effect typed in monospace font.

## 4. Face Cards & Aces (The Universal Powers)
Because J, Q, K, and A share the same text across all suits, their art must represent the core characters of Violet's Revenge.

*   **J - The Lieutenant:** Depicted as a junior coroner or a masked "Guilty" culprit pushing a gurney (pre-staging a Pawn).
*   **Q - The Strategist:** Depicted as Violet in life—standing at her forensics desk, holding case files, calculating her next move.
*   **K - The Warlord:** Depicted as Violet fully transformed. The supernatural Executioner, wearing her purple and black #88 jersey, wielding her hockey stick like a scythe, glowing with purple "Iron Resolve" energy.
*   **A - The Wild Fate:** Depicted as the potted purple plant with the tiny tombstone ("Here lies Violet"). It represents the catalyst—the grave dirt ritual that started it all.

## 5. Example Card Layouts (Art Descriptions)

### [ ♠ 8 ] VANGUARD CHARGE
**[ Art Box: Violet swinging her crooked hockey stick in a wide, violent arc, shattering a morgue tile wall. ]**
**CONQUEST**
**Attack str. 8.** Play with a second Spades card to combine their values.

### [ ♣ 10 ] UNBREAKABLE LINE
**[ Art Box: A massive, heavy steel morgue freezer door slammed shut, with purple supernatural energy failing to breach it. ]**
**FORTIFICATION**
**Block str. 10.** Full block guaranteed regardless of attack value. Discard after use.

### [ ♦ 6 ] SHADOW MAPWORK
**[ Art Box: A dark morgue floor glowing with bright blue Luminol, revealing the invisible footprints of a fleeing culprit. ]**
**INTELLIGENCE**
Force opponent to reveal 1 hidden chess piece's location (not type).

### [ ♥ 2 ] EMBER SPARK
**[ Art Box: A single white candle flickering in the dark on top of a small tombstone. Kindling the flame. ]**
**RESTORATION**
Recover 1 RP. Kindle the flame — small but certain.

## 6. Card Back Design
The back of the deck features the ultimate symbol of the Purge:
*   A pitch-black background.
*   In the center, a large, stylized **Autopsy Slab** (representing the "Hook" from the game).
*   Lying on the slab is Violet's odd-shaped hockey stick, crossed with a forensics scalpel.
*   The edges are framed by the repeating text: *"HERE LIES VIOLET. GONE BUT NOT FORGOTTEN."*
*   The border glows with a faint, supernatural purple.
