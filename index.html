🟣 VIOLET'S REVENGE: THE MORGUE

VIOLET'S REVENGE

PROTOTYPE v0.1 — Game Jam Build

You are THE GUILTY. You wake in a morgue. Collect 3 Case Files and reach the Exit Door.

Violet hunts you. She leaves Nightingale Traps that sing when you pass — revealing your location.

She catches you 3 times... it's the Autopsy Slab.

WASD / Arrow Keys — Move  |  Collect 3 Case Files (🟡)  |  Reach Exit (🚪)  |  Avoid Violet (🟣)
